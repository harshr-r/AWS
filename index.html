About the Fest

Everything you need to know about SR University Fest 2024

SR University Annual Fest

SR University Fest is the largest cultural and technical festival of the university, bringing together students, faculty, and industry experts for three days of celebration, learning, and entertainment.

This year's fest promises to be bigger and better with over 50 events spread across three exciting days. From cultural performances to technical competitions, from celebrity performances to interactive workshops - there's something for everyone!

Join thousands of participants and spectators as we celebrate the spirit of innovation, creativity, and university life.
